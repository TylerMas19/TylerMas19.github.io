Rock, Paper, Scissors

Rules

Rock, Paper, Scissors is the most INTENSE of games. Each player must choose one of the following hands to throw:
  • Rock
  • Paper
  • Scissors

Browser will also choose a hand to throw. If both the player and Browser pick the same hand, it's a tie. Otherwise,
  • Paper beats rock.
  • Scissors beats paper.
  • Rock beat scissors.
For more information, go to the World RPS Society
